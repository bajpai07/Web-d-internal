Environment

Environment, in the literal sense, means the space or situation that surrounds a being. It also refers to the conditions and circumstances in which something or someone’s growth is influenced. The article discusses the main aspects of the environment and the importance of preserving the environment. Go through the topics below to have a better understanding.

Plants

Neem

Neem is an herb from the neem tree, which may have some potential benefits, such as supporting dental health and hair care. However, more research is necessary.

Alovera

Aloe vera is a medicinal plant that grows in hot climates such as California, New Mexico, and the Caribbean. It contains more than 75 active ingredients, including enzymes, amino acids, vitamins, and minerals, some of which could make it useful for treating diseases.

Tulsi

Tulsi in India is ubiquitous in Hindu tradition. Tulsi is derived from a Sanskrit word, which means “matchless one”. Its scientific name is Ocimum Sanctum. It is used in various medicinal systems.

Basil

Basil is a popular herb used in cooking and is known for its medicinal properties. It's used in traditional medicine to treat conditions like indigestion, skin problems, and inflammation.

Lavender

Lavender is widely used in aromatherapy for its calming effects. It also has antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties and can be used to treat anxiety, headaches, and stress.

Chamomile

Chamomile is commonly used in teas for its calming effects. It's also used for its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and mild sedative properties to help with sleep and digestion.
